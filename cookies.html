Spixi.io Cookies Policy


This website, like most other websites, uses cookies. Cookies are messages that web servers send to your web browser when you visit Internet sites, and it helps us provide you with a customized experience while enhancing the functionality of the website. You can freely prevent these cookies from being stored, however this may downgrade certain elements of this websites` functionality. For more general information
on cookies see the  https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTTP_cookie.
You can at any time change or withdraw your consent from the Cookie Policy page on our website. If you fail to receive any information about cookies and the use of cookies on our website from this document, please contact us at support@spixi.io.
 
1. Cookies on our website
This site uses different types of cookies. 

1.1. Cookies based on who is placing them
The following type of cookies are used based on who is placing them:

First-party cookies -  cookies placed by us, to help us provide you with a personalized experience 

Third-party cookies - cookies placed by domains other than www.spixi.io

1.2. Cookies based on expiry times
The following type of cookies are used based on the expiry of each cookie:

Session - cookies are deleted from your computer when you close your browser

Permanent - cookies remain stored on users` computer until deleted or, until they reach expiry date.

2. Use of cookies
Our website uses cookies for multiple purposes. Our site offers newsletter or email subscription services. In this case cookies will be used to remember your subscription, your previous registration and/or whether to show certain notifications which might only be valid to subscribed/unsubscribed users.

If you want to learn more about Google's privacy policy you can read it here: http://www.google.com/privacypolicy.html
